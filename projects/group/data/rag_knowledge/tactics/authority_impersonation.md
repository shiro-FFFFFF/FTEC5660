# Tactic: Authority Impersonation

## Description
The scammer claims to represent police, a bank, government, customs, or another trusted authority.

## Common Language
- police
- investigation
- cybercrime
- security team
- government officer
- official case

## Why It Matters
Victims are more likely to comply with instructions from perceived authority.

## Common Scam Types
- fake police
- fake bank security alert
- tax/government threats
