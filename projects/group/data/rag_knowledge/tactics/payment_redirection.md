# Tactic: Payment Redirection

## Description
The victim is instructed to send money to a new, temporary, safe, or changed account.

## Common Language
- transfer to this account instead
- safe account
- holding account
- secure account
- updated beneficiary details

## Why It Matters
Payment redirection is a direct precursor to financial loss.

## Common Scam Types
- fake police
- supplier impersonation
- urgent transfer scam
