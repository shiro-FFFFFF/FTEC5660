# Tactic: Urgency

## Description
Urgency pushes the victim to act before thinking or verifying.

## Common Language
- urgent
- immediately
- final notice
- act now
- today only
- hurry

## Why It Matters
Urgency reduces reflection and increases compliance.

## Common Scam Types
- phishing
- fake police
- payment diversion
- delivery fee scam
- investment scam
