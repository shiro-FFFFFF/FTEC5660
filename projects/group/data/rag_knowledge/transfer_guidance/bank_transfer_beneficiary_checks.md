# Transfer Guidance: Bank Transfer Beneficiary Checks

## Summary
Bank transfer review should consider both account details and prior context.

## Beneficiary Signals
- recipient name
- account number
- name/account match result
- reported risk status of beneficiary account

## Important Risk Cues
- mismatch between recipient name and registered account name
- only partial / close match
- account has prior risk reports
- first-time recipient
- elevated or large transfer amount

## Interpretation
A beneficiary mismatch or previously reported account materially increases transfer risk, especially when combined with urgency or recent suspicious communications.
