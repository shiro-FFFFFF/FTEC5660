# Transfer Guidance: Contextual Transfer Risk

## Principle
A bank transfer should not be judged in isolation.

## Important Context
- suspicious SMS before transfer
- suspicious call before transfer
- trust-building chat history
- urgency or secrecy across channels
- new recipient
- beneficiary mismatch
- prior risk reports on beneficiary account
- recipient name and bank account consistency

## Why This Matters
A transfer that looks neutral by itself may be high risk when viewed together with prior events.

## Example High-Risk Pattern
- suspicious call
- secrecy instruction
- urgent transfer request
- new recipient
- large amount

## Example Lower-Risk Pattern
- familiar recipient
- expected purpose
- no recent suspicious communications
- no domain or keyword flags
