# Benign Pattern: Utility Bill Payment

## Summary
A normal utility payment is usually routine, expected, and linked to a known biller or standard payment behavior.

## Typical Channels
- SMS reminder
- email
- app notification
- direct payment

## Common Benign Signals
- expected monthly cycle
- known merchant or biller
- ordinary amount
- no secrecy request
- no emotional manipulation
- no unusual transfer urgency
- beneficiary details are stable and recognizable

## Why It Is Lower Risk
The event fits normal household payment behavior and lacks classic scam escalation patterns.

## Caution
A fake overdue bill message can imitate this pattern, so domain, sender, and context still matter.
