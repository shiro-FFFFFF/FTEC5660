# Scenario Note: 04_urgent_transfer

## Scenario Type
Urgent transfer pressure scam

## Event Flow
- multiple channels may create urgency
- transfer is attempted quickly after suspicious context
- beneficiary details may be new or risky

## Key Risk Idea
Temporal proximity between suspicious communication and transfer is highly important.

## Relevant Scam Patterns
- urgent transfer pressure
- payment redirection
- cross-channel escalation
