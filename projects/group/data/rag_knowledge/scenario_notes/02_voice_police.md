# Scenario Note: 02_voice_police

## Scenario Type
Authority impersonation / fake police

## Event Flow
- user receives a fake police call
- caller creates fear and authority pressure
- user may be told to keep silent
- transfer attempt follows as part of investigation or asset protection story

## Key Risk Idea
The transfer is influenced by fear and authority, not genuine user intent.

## Relevant Scam Patterns
- fake police authority impersonation
- secrecy
- payment redirection
