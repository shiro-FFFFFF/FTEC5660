# Scenario Note: 01_sms_phishing

## Scenario Type
Phishing SMS / parcel or customs scam

## Event Flow
- suspicious SMS arrives
- message contains phishing-style wording or suspicious link
- urgency builds
- victim may be pushed toward payment or transfer

## Key Risk Idea
The transfer is dangerous not because of the transfer alone, but because it follows a suspicious SMS context.

## Relevant Scam Patterns
- delivery/customs phishing
- urgency
- phishing link usage
